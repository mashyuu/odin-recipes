Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasonings

Steps

  1. Heat Oven to 350C
  2. Knead out the dough to your desired size.
  3. add a heavy layer of the crushed tomatoes
  4. generously salt the tomatoes on the dough
  5. add your dried herbs
  6. put the pizza in the oven
  7. in the last few minutes of baking add the fresh basil
  8. Cook until golden brown on the bottom and top.
