Vegan Banana Smoothie

Description

A vegan banana smoothie is a creamy, dairy-free blend made from ripe bananas and plant-based ingredients. It typically includes a base of non-dairy milk such as almond, soy, or oat milk, and might be enhanced with other fruits, a splash of vanilla extract, or a handful of greens. Sweetened naturally by the bananas, it's a nutritious and delicious drink thatâ€™s both refreshing and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Plant Based Milk
  • Choice of Sweetener
  • OPTIONAL: Protein Powder

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until well mixed.
  3. Enjoy!
